Site Purpose:

I built this portfolio to prove the skills I’ve gained and to reflect how much I’ve grown. It’s more than a collection of projects — it’s a creative space where I can show how I think, how I learn, and how I solve problems. My goal is to keep building, keep improving, and keep showing up as the kind of developer I’m becoming
